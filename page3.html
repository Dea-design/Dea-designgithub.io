Ada Lovelace(1815-1852)

Ada Lovelace is regarded as the world's first computer scientist. In simple terms, computer scientists use their knowledge to test, develop, and design software and computing systems.

Most importantly, she is known for working on Charles Babbage's general- purpose computer, an analytical machine

Her job with Charles was basically to translate an artical by Luigi Menabrea about his machine, and to do that, she created the first computer programn.

Ada had a vision that computers had the capabillity to go beyond calculating equations, while others like Babbage would only focus on those qualities.

Her name has also been used in programming languages, institutes, lectures and courses.

Did you know? - That Ada Lovelace had many famous acquaintances such as Michael Faraday and Charles Darwin!
